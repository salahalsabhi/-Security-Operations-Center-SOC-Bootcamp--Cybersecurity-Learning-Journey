# 📖 Overview

This repository documents everything I learned during the **Security Operations Center (SOC) Bootcamp** organized by **ThinkCloudly**.

Throughout five intensive days, the bootcamp focused on the daily responsibilities of SOC analysts, modern defensive security operations, incident response workflows, cyber threat detection, SIEM technologies, malware analysis, and career preparation for blue team professionals.

The training combined theoretical knowledge with practical demonstrations and real-world cybersecurity scenarios, providing a strong foundation for aspiring SOC Analysts.

# 💡 Key Takeaways

- A Security Operations Center is the backbone of organizational cyber defense.
- Continuous monitoring significantly reduces incident detection and response times.
- SIEM solutions provide centralized visibility across enterprise environments.
- Threat intelligence enables defenders to identify attacker behaviors more effectively.
- Malware analysis helps defenders understand adversary capabilities.
- Incident response requires structured procedures to minimize business impact.
- Log analysis remains one of the most valuable skills for SOC analysts.
- Understanding attacker methodology improves defensive capabilities.
- Modern cybersecurity depends on proactive monitoring rather than reactive defense.
